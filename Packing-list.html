Packing List

Here is a basic packing list to aid you as you get ready for your big adventure

  1. Documents
    • Passport and ID
    • Ship boarding pass
    • Luggage tags
    • Plane tickets
  2. Important considerations
    • Pack for the climate. Check the link to track the weather conditions during our cruise
    • Think about your excursions. Pack appropriate clothing and shoes to match the activity
    • Some items are best put in a carry on bag that will stay with you boarding on the ship. (See Travis' travel tips)
  3. Clothing
    • Socks and underwear
    • Shirts, hoodies and possibly a heavier jacket
    • Pants and shorts
    • Shoes (walking, flip flops and dress)
    • Pajamas
    • Formal attire. One of the dinners is Captain's Night which requires formal attire. It's also a great photo oppritunity with photographers and booths everywhere.
    • Swimsuits
  4. Toiletries
    • Medication. (I reccomend Dramamine and Advil in addition to any required medication so a great vacation does not get ruined by a little motion sickness)
    • Sunscreen and lotion
    • Deodorant
    • Toothpaste, tooth brush and floss
    • Lip balm
    • Shampoo, conditioner and body wash (The ship does provide these much like hotels, but it is low quality stuff)
    • Makeup / Remover
    • Hair brushes / combs / blow dryer/ curler etc.
    • Wet wipes
    • Disposabale razors and shaving cream
  5. Electronics and accessories
    • Phone chargers
    • Headphones
    • Power bank
    • Lanyard (great for cruise cards)
  6. Other items
    • Small day pack for excursions
    • Reusable water bottle (Highly reccomend for travel)
    • Reading material
    • Cash (there will be a casino and lots of shopping on board, as well as, shopping at the ports)
    • Binoculars for viewing wild life as we sail to coast
    • Warm blanket for sitting outside enjoying the view
    • Glasses, sunglasses, contacts etc.
